Sri Lankan Pan Cake

Sri Lankana pancakes are stuffed with sweet coconut. It's also known as pol pani pancake. This is not similar to normal pancake. Thses are very delicious snack for tea time.

Ingredients

Sweet coconut filling(Pani Pol)

  • 90g grated fresh coconut
  • 100g Sugar or jaggery
  • 50ml water
  • l tea spoon salt(taste the filling and adjust as you like)
  • 4 cardamom

Pancake Batter

  • 200g flour
  • 150ml water or coconut milk
  • 1 egg or 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1/4 teasppon tumeric


How To Prepare
  1. Step 01 - Make the filling
    • Open the shell of cardamom pods and grind them to a powder
    • Into a pan, add fresh grated coconut and roast. Do not wait till they get brown.
    • In a pan, add sugar, powdere cardamom, salt with 2tbsp of water.
    • Mix well, turn on the heat and let the sugar dissolve and heat up.Don't mixing..
    • Turn off the heat when the sugar is completely dissolved.
    • Then add dried coconut to the syrup and mix well. Let the mixture cool down.
  2. Step 02 - Make the pancake batter
    • In bowl, add flour,oil or egg, coconut milk and tumeric. Then blend it to get a smooth lump-free batter.
    • Add a flour if the batter is too runny and add abit of coconut milk if the batter is too thick.(It should be little thicker than whipping cream)
    • Heat a non stick frying pan and add 2 drops of oil.
    • Add 1/3 cup of batter to the pan and swirl it around to spread it. Let the pancake cook until it looks cooked all the way through.
  3. Step 03 - Assembly
    • Place the pancake on a flat surface, scoop 1 tbsp of the mixture in the middle, fold both sides, then roll to seal.
    • Repeat the process for the rest of the mixture and pancakes
    • Enjoy with a cup of tea.
