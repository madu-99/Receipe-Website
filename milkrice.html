Sri Lankan Milk Rice

Milk Rice or Kiribath is a traditional Sri Lankan dish and it is prepared by rice with coconut milk. It is very commonly served for the breaksfast. So, Sri Lankans are made milk rice for their special occasions. Such as, Sinhala and Tamil New Year, Birthdays and their special days of the life.

Milk Rice is usually served with lunumiris. So, Let's see how to prepare milk rice.

Ingredients
  • White rice - 500g
  • Thickened coconut milk - 2 Cups
  • Water - 2 Cups
  • Salt to taste


How To Prepare
  1. Step 01
    • Wash the rice and put into the rice cooker.
  2. Step 02
    • Add water until it covers the rice and cooking until the rice is soft about 26-20 minutes.
  3. Step 03
    • Add coconut milk and salt, stir well.
  4. Step 04
    • Reduce heat to low and cooking for another 10-15 minutes until the cocount milk has been absorbed.
  5. Step 05
    • The rice is extremely smooth and soft after the coconut milk has been absorbed. Rice milk is now ready.
  6. Step 06
    • Put the milk rice on a tray and cut it into pieces.
