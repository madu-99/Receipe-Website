Pan Cake

Pan cakes are very delicious and you can customize them with your favourite add-ins. It is so light and fluffy. You only need a few simple ingredients.

The secret to light and fluffy pancakes is baking powder. It reacts with the heat of the pan and helps the pancake rise.

The boxed pancake mix is going away with you. Most likely, you already have everything in your kitchen to cook great pancakes. It's simple to make these pancakes!

Ingredients
  • 25g melt butter
  • 3/4 cup luckwarm milk
  • 150g flour
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1/2 tsp vanila extract
  • 1 3/4 or 2 tsp baking powder


How To Prepare
  1. Step 01
    • Whisk flour, sugar, baking powder and the salt in a bowl
  2. Step 02
    • Whisk milk, egg, melted butter and vanila extract until combined.
  3. Step 03
    • Heat a pan over medium heat. The pan is ready if when you splatter a little water onto the pan surface, the water dances around the pan and eventually evaporates.
  4. Step 04
    • Pour mixture and use a fork to stir until you no longer see clumps of flour. it's okay if the batter has small lumps - it's important not to over-mix the batter.
  5. Step 05
    • Use a 1/4-cup measuring cup to spoon batter onto the skillet. Gently spread the batter into a 4-inch circle.
  6. Step 06
    • When edges look dry, and bubble start to appear and pop on the top surfaces of the pancake, turn over. This takes about 2 minutes. Once flipped, cook another 1-2 minutes or until lightly browned and cooked in the middle.
  7. Step 07
    • Serve immediately with warm syrup, butter and berries.
