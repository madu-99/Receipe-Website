Butter Cake

Butter cake is very delicious. It can be made for many uses and frosted if you like. We can eat butter cake as a morning snack or evening tea. As well as, you can make for special occasion.

It is so easy to make a butter cake. But the process of making the butter cake that makes it a good cake or bad cake.

Ingredients
  • 250g Butter
  • 250g Sugar
  • 250g Flour or cake flour
  • 2 tsp Baking powder
  • 4 Eggs
  • 2 tsp Vanila essence
  • 4 tbsp milk


How To Prepare
  1. Step 01
    • Preheat oven to 170 0C.
  2. Step 02
    • Firstly mix sugar and butter using cake mixer on high speed until creamy.
  3. Step 03
    • Add in the eggs one by one until fully incorporated. Then, add vanil and mix well.
  4. Step 04
    • Reduce the speed and fold in the sifted flour and baking powder.
  5. Step 05
    • Finally, add milk and mix until fully incoporated.
  6. Step 06
    • Add batter to the prepared tray and bake in the oven for 35-40 minutes.
  7. Step 07
    • Take it out of the cake pans and let it cool on a wire rack.
  8. Step 08
    • Once cool down, cut into square pieces and enjoy.
